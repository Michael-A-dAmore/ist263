Angotti's Family Timeline

1910

Humble Beginnings

Roberto Angotti's Grandfather was from a small town in Sicily he decided to take a big step and leave for America and live the American dream

1932

A new challenge

After arriving in the US. Mr. Angotti decided to settle into a small apartment in NYC where he worked in construction.

1948

Passion ignites

After WWII Mr. Angottis decides to open a little restaurant in NYC cooking his best dishes from his small town in Sicily. Teaching his son so one day the buisness would be passed on

1955

After a few years they decided to move due to NYC rising prices. They finally settled down in Syracuse and opened a family friendly Italian Restaurant.The restaurant was passed down to his son and later to his grandson, Roberto Angotti.
